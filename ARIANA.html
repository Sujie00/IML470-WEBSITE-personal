ARIANA GRANDE

Ariana Grande-Butera is an American singer and actress known for her four-octave vocal range and whistle register. Regarded as a pop icon, she has won numerous awards, including two Grammys and 36 Guinness World Records. Grande began her career at 15 in the Broadway musical "13" and rose to fame as Cat Valentine on Nickelodeon's "Victorious" and "Sam & Cat." She signed with Republic Records in 2011 and released her debut album "Yours Truly" in 2013. Her second album, "My Everything" (2014), included hits like "Problem" and "Bang Bang," while her third album, "Dangerous Woman" (2016), cemented her success.
