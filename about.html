WHY I LIKE MUSIC......

I like music because it has a way of connecting with my emotions like nothing else. When I listen to my favourite songs, I feel a sense of calm and happiness wash over me. For instance, when I'm stressed after a long day of studying, playing soothing acoustic tracks helps me unwind and relax. Music also plays a significant role in my studies. Listening to instrumental or classical music while studying helps me concentrate better. It creates a pleasant background ambience that keeps me focused and motivated. Additionally, some subjects, like languages and history, become more engaging when I explore related music genres, helping me understand cultural contexts better. One of the most profound ways music impacts my life is through my religious practices. Listening to religious hymns and songs deepens my spiritual connection and brings peace and reverence. These songs often carry potent messages and teachings, reinforcing my faith and values. Singing in a choir or participating in musical worship services also fosters a sense of community and shared belief, which is incredibly fulfilling.
